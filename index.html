Wages and salaries of workers in 1400

The minimum daily wage is the basis for receiving the insurance premiums of the insured subject to labor and social security laws (excluding jobs and activities with a lump sum wage) ‌ ۰۰ 1400 onwards, amounting to 855,165 Rials. Accordingly, with a 39% increase in the minimum wage in 1400, a minimum wage worker with one child will receive a total of 4,111,417 tomans per month.

  • The minimum daily wage is equal to 855,165 Rials
  • The minimum monthly wage (30 days) is equal to 26,554,925 Rials
  • Allowance for household items equivalent to 6,000,000 Rials
  • Housing allowance equal to 4,500,000 Rials (It should be noted that the right to housing is 450,000 Tomans after the approval of the Cabinet.)
  • Child rights (per child) equal to 2,655,492 Rial
  • Basic years (provided you have one year of work experience) daily equivalent to 46,667 Rials

How to calculate the benefits of labor law in year 1400

  • Wage calculation formula per working hour: daily wage divided by 7.33 (seven and thirty three hundredths)
  • Labor Law Overtime Calculation Formula: Hourly Wage Multiplied by 1.4 (One and Fourteenth) Multiplied by Overtime Hours
  • Labor Law Friday Job Calculation Formula: Hourly Wage Multiplied by 0.4 (Fourteenth) Multiplied by Friday Working Hours
  • Labor Law night work calculation formula: hourly wage multiplied by 0.35 (thirty five percent) multiplied by night work hours
  • Formula for calculating annual years: Work experience in years multiplied by monthly salary
  • Monthly years calculation formula: Work experience per month multiplied by 2.5 (two and a half) times the daily wage of the worker
  • Formula for calculating Eid and annual bonus: Work experience per year multiplied by the last monthly salary
  • Monthly years calculation formula: Work experience per month multiplied by 5 times the last daily wage of the worker
